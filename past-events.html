Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, CDSR, was held on August 7 - 9, 2017, in Toronto, Canada. Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Dr. Agamemnon Crassidis
    for the paper entitled: "A Model-Free Control System Based on the Sliding Mode Control Method with Applications to Multi-Input-Multi-Output Systems"
  • Cassidy Westin
    for the paper entitled: "Cable-Pulley Interaction with Dynamic Wrap Angle Using the Absolute Nodal Coordinate Formulation"
  • Mahdokht Ezati
    for the paper entitled: "Optimal Vertical Jump of a Human"
  • Mojtaba Hedayatpour
    for the paper entitled: "Revised Propeller Dynamics and Energy-Optimal Hovering in a Monospinner"
  • Dr. Nezam Mahdavi-Amiri
    for the paper entitled: "A New Error in Variables Model for Solving Positive Semi-Definite Linear Systems with Applications to Well-known Control Problems"
