Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Jian-Feng Shi
    for the paper entitled: "Level-set and Image Statistics for Pose Estimation of Satellites"
  • Achini Herath
    for the paper entitled: "Timetabling with Three-Parent Genetic Algorithm: a Preliminary Study"
  • Dr. Mohammad Habibur Rahman
    for the paper entitled: "A New Integral Second-Order Terminal Sliding Mode Control with Time Delay Estimation for an Exoskeleton Robot with Dynamics Uncertainties"
