CDSR 2017:

CDSR 2017 will be held in Toronto, Canada on August 21 - 23, 2017.

CDSR'17 Workshop

International ASET Inc. is proud to present Dr. Goldie Nejat and Dr. Mojtaba Ahmadi as the presenters of our workshop for the 4th International Conference on Control, Dynamic Systems, and Robotics (CDSR'17)

Topic of Workshop: The Future of Assistive Robots for Social and Physical Interactions

The workshop will be held in the afternoon of August 22, 2017. All conference attendees are welcome to attend the workshop free of charge. The registration fee for non-conference attendees will be 113 CAD (HST included). Workshop attendees will receive a certificate of participation. For registration, please visit: here.

CDSR'17 Symposiums:

The Organizing Committee has selected the following fields for specialized and focused symposiums under the umbrella of CDSR'17:

  • Intelligent Manufacturing and Automation
  • UAV Systems
  • Control theory: Linear, Nonlinear, Learning, and Adaptive
  • Mechatronics and Robotics
  • Healthcare, Medical, and Assistive Robotics
  • Sensing, Instrumentation, and Measurement
  • 3D printing technologies
  • Human-machine and Human-robot Interaction

If you are interested of your article to be part of the above-mentioned symposiums, please inform us when submitting your article. Please visit Symposiums for more information.
