Biomechatronics and Biorobotics

This symposium focuses on biomedical/clinical research and biomechatronic technologies for assistance, assessment, and recovery from motor disabilities. The symposium will host a number of distinguished scholars with complementary expertise in biomedical technology, neuroscience, physical therapy, and knowledge translation. Through presentations and moderated discussions, the symposium will identify research questions of common interest for the creation of a research plan of international relevance.
